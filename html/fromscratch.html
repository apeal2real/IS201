From Scratch

Directions:

  1. Gather ingredients (Makes 14 large cookies or 16-18 medium sized):
    • 1/2 cup (100g) Brown sugar, packed
    • 1/4 cup (50g) White sugar
    • 1/2 cup (115g) Unsalted butter, softened
    • 1 large Egg
    • 2 teaspoons Vanilla extract
    • 1½ (190g) All-purpose flour
    • 3/4 teaspoon Baking soda
    • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
    • 1 cup (160g) Chocolate chips or less if you prefer
  2. In a large bowl, beat softened butter, brown sugar and white sugar. Beat until creamy, about 2 minutes.
  3. Add egg, vanilla extract and beat until combined, scrape the bottom and sides as needed.
  4. In a separate bowl mix flour, baking soda and salt.
  5. Add flour mixture into the butter mixture. 1/2 at the time, mix until combined.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. At this stage if dough is too soft, cover and refrigerate for 20 minutes.
  8. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). line two baking trays with parchment paper.
  9. Scoop the dough onto a prepared baking sheet, leaving at least 3 inches (7.5 cm) of space between the cookies.
  10. Refrigerate for 30-40 minutes.
  11. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until slightly golden around the edges.
  12. Allow to cool before serving.
